## What UCH Is

Unity Care Hospital (UCH) is a modular, sovereign-ready telehealth infrastructure platform engineered for hospital groups, healthcare operators, and national health systems that require **full deployment control**, compliance-aligned architecture, and long-term autonomy — without reliance on external SaaS ecosystems.

UCH is not a subscription product. It is deployed as a **dedicated institutional instance**, contracted as a license, full IP transfer, or sovereign national stack.

---

## The Problem UCH Solves

Healthcare institutions face three structural risks with SaaS telehealth:

1. **Vendor lock-in** — pricing, roadmap, and data residency controlled by a third party
2. **Compliance exposure** — cross-jurisdiction data hosting complicates national and regional regulations
3. **Exit barriers** — high migration costs and integration complexity trap institutions in unfavorable contracts

UCH eliminates these risks at the contract and infrastructure level.
