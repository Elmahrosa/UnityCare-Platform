## 1. Purpose

## 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party including but not limited to:

- Source code, architecture diagrams, and technical documentation
- Financial projections, pricing, and commercial terms
- Security documentation and audit reports
- Customer pipeline, deployment topology, and operational data
- Any materials marked "Confidential" or reasonably understood to be confidential

---

## 3. Exclusions

Information is NOT considered confidential if it:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party
- Was independently developed by the receiving party without reference to disclosed information
- Was lawfully received from a third party without restriction
- Was already known to the receiving party prior to disclosure

---

## 4. Obligations

Each receiving party shall:

- Use Confidential Information only for the Purpose stated above
- Restrict access to employees and agents with a demonstrable need to know
- Not disclose to any third party without prior written approval
- Apply at least the same standard of care as it applies to its own confidential information (no less than reasonable care)

---

## 5. Term

This Agreement is effective upon signature and confidentiality obligations survive for **3 (three) years** from the date of last disclosure.

---

## 6. No License

No intellectual property rights, licenses, or ownership interests are granted by the disclosure of Confidential Information under this Agreement.

---

## 7. Return of Materials

Upon written request, each party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information received, and certify destruction in writing if requested.

---

## 8. Governing Law
