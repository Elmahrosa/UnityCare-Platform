# Acquisition & Onboarding Process

## Licensing Model
- **Recurring SaaS License**: Annual institutional subscription
- **Usage Rights**: Tiered by patient volume
- **Compliance Guarantees**: Audit trail, legal defensibility

## Buyer Journey
1. **NDA Access** → Secure institutional kit
2. **Pilot Deployment** → Limited scope hospital rollout
3. **Compliance Audit** → External verification
4. **Full Adoption** → National health system integration

## Anchor Targets
- Banks (health insurance integration)
- Civic data platforms
- National health ministries
