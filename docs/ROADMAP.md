# Product Roadmap

The Unity Care Hospital platform is under continuous development with a focus on expanding healthcare infrastructure capabilities.

---

## Phase 1 — Core Platform

Completed capabilities include:

- patient management
- telemedicine consultations
- appointment scheduling
- electronic prescriptions
- pharmacy integration
- doctor dashboards
- administrative control panel

---

## Phase 2 — AI Assisted Healthcare

Planned improvements include:

- AI assisted medical triage
- diagnostic decision support
- automated documentation assistance
- healthcare analytics dashboards

---

## Phase 3 — IoT Healthcare Integration

Future integrations include:

- wearable health device connectivity
- remote patient monitoring
- hospital equipment telemetry
- automated alerts for medical staff

---

## Phase 4 — National Health Infrastructure

Long-term platform expansion targets include:

- national healthcare registries
- government health data networks
- cross hospital patient record exchange
- secure healthcare identity systems

---

## Development Philosophy

The platform roadmap focuses on:

- healthcare interoperability
- scalable infrastructure
- security and privacy
- institutional deployment readiness
